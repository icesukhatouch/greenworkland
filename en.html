Our vision

Greenworkland Co., ltd. focuses on Industrial Estate Development as well as Standard Industrial Construction for purchase and rental in Bangkok’s industrial areas under the concept of ‘Eco Industrial Town’. We work with awareness that everything needs to be managed to preserve the environment for the quality and welfare of the people. We want to bring harmony to the locals and community around the industrial areas. Our company is developing a project called ‘Greenwork Industrial Park Suksawat-Pracha Uthit’ located on the purple industrial area which covers 143 rai or 229,528.24 square meters on the Pracha Uthit-Wat Khu Sang road in Samut Prakan Province. We also have plans to expand the development of eco-industrial areas to bring the balance to other industrial areas in the future.

    Thanon Kanchanaphisek

    Bangkok’s southern outer ring or the so called ‘Thanon Kanchanaphisek’ is just 6 kilometers from the Greenwork Industrial Park. The road connects the eastern part of Bangkok such as Bangna, Suvarnabhumi Airport and also the western part such as Thanon Praram 2.

    Bhumibol Bridge

    The bridge is also known as Mega Bridge, is 9 kilometers away from the location. The bridge joined Samut Prakan and southern part of Bangkok together. This route is the best route to commute to the economic area of Sathorn and Bangkok Port (Khlong Toei).

    Chalerm Maha Nakhon Expressway

    11 kilometers away from the spot is Chalerm Maha Nakhon Expressway which can take you to all routes in Bangkok in no time.

    Siam Suksawat Terminal (SSW)

    Siam Suksawat Terminal is the provider of full-scale container services include; shipping, docking, delivery, and saving the containers and its contents. The terminal is just 6 kilometers from the Greenwork Industrial Park.

    Bangkok Modern Terminal (BMTP)

    Bangkok Modern Terminal is also a one-stop container service which includes taxation of imports and exports by Customs Department inside the port, this terminal is only 9 kilometers from the location.

    Suvarnabhumi Airport

    Suvarnabhumi Airport is just 47 kilometres away from the site. The close distance makes international shipping more convenient which is the key for global expansion.
